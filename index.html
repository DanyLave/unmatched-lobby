How to Play
Basic Gameplay
1. Draw Phase: Tap the draw pile to draw cards into your hand. Your hand shows all available cards.
2. Play Phase: Select cards from your hand to stage them face-down. When ready, tap "Play" to reveal all staged cards at once.
3. Discard Phase: After revealing, you can discard cards or return them to your hand. Discarded cards go to the discard pile (or intermediate zone if your deck has one).
Zones Explained
Draw Pile
Your deck of cards. Tap to draw one card, or use "Browse" to see all cards and pick specific ones.
Hand
Cards you've drawn. Tap any card to see options (stage, discard, return to deck). Use "Select Cards" to work with multiple cards at once.
Staged
Cards ready to be played. They appear face-down to other players. Tap "Play" to reveal them all at once with a flip animation.
Intermediate Zone
(Only some decks have this) A special zone between hand and discard. When you discard cards, they go here first. Tap any card to move it to hand, discard, or deck.
Discard Pile
Cards that have been played and discarded. You can browse them or shuffle them back into the draw pile.
